Lasagna

Yummy Lasagna guaranteed to make your mouth water

Ingredients

  • Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodes
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • 1 egg
